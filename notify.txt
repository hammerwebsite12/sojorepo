🛠️ Steam Update ERROR Loop Fix 
Stuck on a "Steam Updating" loop or steam not booting/starting? Here is the quick fix:

Open Hammer App → Click Unlock Mode 2 🔓

Ignore errors → Open Steam

Steam Settings > Downloads > Clear Download Cache 🧹

Restart Steam

Close & Re-open Hammer → Click the Red Circle to reactivate Unlock Mode 🔴

Restart Steam again and you're all set! 🚀
