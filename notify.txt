🛠️ STEAM FIX 3/17/26 : Purchase Errors & Missing Games (Hammer 3.4) 🚀
If you’re seeing purchase errors or your library looks empty
Follow these steps exactly:
1️⃣ Unlock Mode: Open Hammer 3.4 and click UNLOCK MODE 2.
(If an error message or window pops up, just ignore it and close that window.)
2️⃣ Exit: Close the Hammer app completely.
3️⃣ Run as Admin: Re-open the Hammer app (Right-click the icon and select "Run as Administrator"). 🛡️
4️⃣ Re-Activate: Click the Red Circle to activate UNLOCK MODE again.
5️⃣ Finish: Click Restart Steam when prompted. Your games should now be visible and errors cleared! ✅
