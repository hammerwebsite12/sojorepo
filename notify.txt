⚠️ IMPORTANT UPDATE: MISSING GAMES OR PURCHASE ERRORS ⚠️
If you have updated your Steam client today 3/10/26 and are noticing that games are missing from your library or showing up as "Purchase" instead of "Play," you are not alone! This is a known issue caused by the latest app update.

THE FIX:
To restore your library and fix the "missing games" bug, follow these steps:

1️⃣  Open Steam and go to Settings.
2️⃣  Select the Downloads tab.
3️⃣  Click Clear Download Cache.
4️⃣  Steam will restart.

After the restart, your games should reappear, and the "Play" buttons will be restored! ✅
