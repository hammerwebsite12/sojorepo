🛠️ STEAM FIX 3/17/26 : Purchase Errors & Missing Games (Hammer 3.4) 🚀
If you’re seeing purchase errors or your library looks empty, follow these steps exactly:
1 Unlock Mode: Open Hammer 3.4 and click UNLOCK MODE 2.
(Ignore and close any error pop-ups.)
2 Exit: Close the Hammer app completely.
3 Run as Admin: Re-open Hammer (Right-click icon > Run as Administrator). 🛡️
4 Re-Activate: Click the Red Circle to activate UNLOCK MODE again.
5 Clear Cache (If needed): Still bugged? Go to Steam > Settings > Downloads > Clear Download Cache. 💡
Finish: Click Restart Steam. Your games should now be visible! ✅
